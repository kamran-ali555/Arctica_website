Secure Asset Fund for Users

The only protocol that allows you to use the reference implementation to create a multisignature wallet, with an easily accessible GUI and minimal additional software. No hardware wallets, real airgaps, and protection from supply chain attacks and advanced BIOS attacks.

Personalised Access Control

YOU decide who participates in your multisignature. We never have any control or access to your funds.

Advanced Data Encryption

Multisignature fixes the 'security' issue of traditional bitcoin storage, but does not solve the 'privacy' issue (the fact that an Adversary who discovers a single backup can see your balance and entire spending history). Thanks to our encryption scheme (Reading Keys), we have fixed this issue.
